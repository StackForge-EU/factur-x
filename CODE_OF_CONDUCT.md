# Code of Conduct

## Our Values

We expect participants to uphold **respect**, **collaboration**, and **constructive feedback**. Assume good intent and help others learn.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful of differing viewpoints and experience levels
- Stay on topic in issues and pull requests
- Give constructive, actionable feedback
- Welcome newcomers

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Discriminatory or offensive language
- Deliberate disruption or derailing of discussions
- Dismissing others' contributions without constructive input

## Enforcement

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it to **conduct@stack-forge.eu**.

Maintainers will review reports, take appropriate action, and, where needed, communicate outcomes to the involved parties.
